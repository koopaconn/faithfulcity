About Us

Connecting people to churches

Finding a church were Jesus followers can thrive is important. At Faithful City we want to connect people to churches regardless of denomination. Because one of the most important parts of being a Chrisitan is fellowship.

Find the lost in the city

As Christian we should be driven to seek and save the lost. At Faithful City we strive to find the lost in our city and connect them with a church. We do this by creating tools that make it easier to find a church they can feel at home in.

Help those in need

Christians should have a heart for the needy. Creating tools to connect people is the first part of our goals. A future goal is to unite the church is the Kansas City area to make a bigger difference that any one church could. Combining resources and time to impact our city.
